# Q5. Draw a flowchart to find the odd and even numbers in a range.

### Algorithm: Find the odd and even numbers in a range.

Step 1: Read numbers a, b

Step 2: Repeat for i = a to b+1

    Check i % 2 == 0
        YES:
            Display i, "is even"
        NO:
            Display i, "is odd"

Step 3: END ALGO
